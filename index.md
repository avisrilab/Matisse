Integrative analysis of single-cell transcriptomics with isoform
resolution. Matisse extends Seurat and Signac to support PSI (Percent
Spliced In) matrix calculation, isoform-aware quality control, and
visualization for long-read and splice-resolved single-cell RNA-seq
data. It provides a dedicated S4 object that wraps a Seurat object,
keeping gene expression and isoform layers synchronized throughout an
analysis.
